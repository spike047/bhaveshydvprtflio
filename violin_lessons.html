🎻 Violin Strings & Notes

The violin has four strings, tuned to the following notes:

G String:    G  A  B  C  D  
D String:    D  E  F# G  A  
A String:    A  B  C# D  E  
E String:    E  F# G# A  B

🎵 Basic Violin Chords (Double Stops)

Here are some common double-stop chord combinations:

G+D:   G and D played together  
D+A:   D and A played together  
A+E:   A and E played together

🎵 Advanced Violin Chords

Here are some more complex double stops and triple stops:

G+D+A:   G, D, and A played together  
D+A+E:   D, A, and E played together  
A+E+C#:  A, E, and C# played together
