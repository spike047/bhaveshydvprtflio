My Story

Where tradition
meets artistry

I'm Suman — a mehendi artist based in Mumbai with over 5+ years of experience creating intricate henna designs for weddings, festivals, and special occasions. My journey began tracing patterns on paper before I ever touched a cone.

Every design I create is a dialogue between ancient tradition and personal expression. I specialize in bridal mehendi, infusing each work with motifs that tell your love story — from the hidden groom's name to the tiniest lotus that symbolises new beginnings.

I use only 100% organic, chemical-free henna paste made fresh for each appointment, because your skin deserves nothing less than pure.

How It Works

From inquiry to beautiful

01
Consultation

Share your occasion, style preferences, and any references. We discuss the design, timeline, and finalise the look together.

02
Booking

A small advance confirms your date. I block my calendar exclusively for you — no rush, no overlapping appointments.

03
The Session

I arrive on time, set up my workspace, and begin. Just relax while the henna takes shape — music, chai, conversation optional.

04
Aftercare

I guide you on drying, sealing with sugar-lemon, and best practices for a deep, lasting stain. Your stain, your story.
